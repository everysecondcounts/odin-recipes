Baked rice with chickpeas

Description

Surfing the web I've bumped into Elaine's delicious "Fluffy Baked Rice with Chickpeas and Crispy Tofu Feta" recipe.

In her own words, it is an:

Easy baked rice with chickpeas, green olives and lots of fresh herbs. Served with crispy marinated tofu feta, perfect for a vegan weeknight dinner or healthy comfort meal.

Ingredients

These are the basic ingredients for this dish

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 block tofu feta
  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup green olives
  • Olive oil
  • Chopped herbs

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 220ºC
    2. In a pan, sauté the onion for 20 min
    3. Add the garlic, the tofu and the chickpeas
    4. Transfer the pan contents to a baking dish
    5. Add the rice
    6. Pour over 3 1/2 cups of boiling water
    7. Bake for 30 min
    8. Enjoy!
