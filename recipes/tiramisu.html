Vegan tiramisu

Description

This is one of the best plant-based tiramisu recipes I've ever tried, perfect for vegan and diary-free diets. However, it's so tasty that anyone will enjoy it!

Ingredients

These are the basic ingredients for the dessert:

  • Vegan ladyfingers
  • Plant-based cream cheese
  • Sweetener
  • Coconut oil
  • Coconut milk
  • Espresso
  • Kahlua

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220ºC
  2. Make the creamy filling by mixing the cream cheese with coconut oil and milk and freeze it during 20 min
  3. Stir the espresso, coffee liquor and sweetener in a bowl
  4. Dip the vegan ladyfingers in the espresso mixture
  5. Assemble the layers, alternating between ladyfingers and plant-based mascarpone
  6. Refrigerate overnight
  7. Enjoy!

For more detail, or if you want to make your ladyfingers from scratch, check out the full recipe on Nora's site)
