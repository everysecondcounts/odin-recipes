Plant-based Greek salad

Description

A super simple, yummy recipe for summer days.

Ingredients

These are the basic ingredients for the salad:

  • 1 lettuce
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup vegan feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • 1/2 dried tomatoes

Steps

  1. Chop all the ingredients (except for the chickpeas)
  2. Mix them in a big bowl
  3. Add olive oil
  4. Enjoy!
